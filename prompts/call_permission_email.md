# Call Permission Email Template

## Purpose
Sent ONLY when a prospect has replied positively to an email sequence.
This email asks for their permission before any phone call is made.

## Rules
- Maximum 3 sentences
- Acknowledge their positive reply in the first sentence
- Ask explicitly: "Would it be okay if I gave you a quick call to find a time
  that works between yourself and our CEO, Declan?"
- End with a flexibility signal: "Happy to work around your schedule."

## Template
Subject: Re: [original subject]

[Warm acknowledgment of their reply, 1 sentence.]
Would it be okay if I gave you a quick call to find a time that works
between yourself and our CEO, Declan?
Happy to work around your schedule.

[Sender name]
